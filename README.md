### 2. Decoding the Magic (Prompt Analysis)

Sometimes, you don't want to just watch. You want to understand *how*. Maybe you want to know how to create "A cinematic drone shot of a coral reef made of paper" or "A Victorian lady riding a motorcycle."

This is where our **Prompt Laboratory** shines. We don't just show you the video; we deconstruct the DNA behind it. We display the exact prompts used to generate these masterpieces. Unlike standard video sites that hide the metadata, ours is fine-tuned for the learner.

It's about education. It's about creating the visual evidence for a story that hasn't been told yet, and teaching you the spell words to cast it yourself.
