## What is this?

We realized that accessing top-tier AI video models like OpenAI's Sora 2 is still a pain. You either have to wait on a waitlist, manage complex developer accounts, or deal with regional restrictions.

**Sora2Web solves that.**

We built a streamlined web interface that hooks directly into the official APIs of the best video models out there. We handle the heavy lifting, the API keys, and the backend infrastructure. You just type a prompt and get a video.

2.  **Feature Requests:** Want us to add a new model (like Runway or Pika)? Let us know.

## How it works (Under the hood)

For the curious nerds: We act as a proxy layer. When you hit generate on our site, we route your request through our optimized backend directly to the respective official provider (e.g., OpenAI or Google). We don't train the models; we just make them accessible to everyone, instantly.
